Chicken Schnitzel

Description

This delicious Golden brown schnitzel will have guests begging you for more. With it's crunchy shell and tender interior there won t be many who can withstand this beauty

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Breading
  • Eggs
  • Flour

Instructions

  1. put the chicken breast in a plastic bag and flatten it until it is about 2cm thick
  2. let the chicken rest until it is room temp
  3. while the chicken is resting whisk the eggs until there are no flecks and place the flour and breading into two large bowls
  4. cover the chicken in flour, eggs and then breading in that order and let sit to dry
  5. while the chicken is drying, heat up cooking oil in a pan to medium-high heat
  6. finally cook the chicken, flipping once when the bottom is golden brown and takinog out when the whole schnitzel is golden brown
